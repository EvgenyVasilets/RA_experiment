In each trial you will see two values that will periodically appear on the screen. One of these values will be the potential loss (it will have the "-" sign), another one - potential gain. You will have an equal chance (50%) to either win the potential gain or loose the potential loss.

In every trial, after the demonstration of the values, you will need to make a decision whether you accept or reject the gamble with these values. Using your keyboard, press "↑" (upwards arrow) to accept the gamble or "↓" (downwards arrow) to reject it.

At the end of the experiment, only one accepted trial will affect your final payoff, which will also include unconditional 10 EUR. This trial will be chosen randomly from all the trials that you accepted. The first {{Constants.num_practice_rounds}} trials are for training so they will not affect your final payoff.

Values that you will see are in the Experimental Currency Units (ECU). 1 ECU = 0.19 EUR.
