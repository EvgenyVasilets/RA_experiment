In each trial you will see two values (gains and losses) that will periodically appear on the screen.

These values are Experimental Currency Unit (ECU). 1 ECU = 0.19 EUR.

You have an equal chance (50%) to either win the gain value or loose the loss value. The loss value has the "-" sign.

Your task is to make a decision after the presentation of the values. Press "a" to accept the gamble or "l" to reject it.

In the end of the experiment, only one trial where you have accepted the gamble will affect your final payoff.

The first {{Constants.num_practice_rounds}} trials are for practice so you will not gain or lose any money.

Good job!

Now the real trials will begin.

Remember to press "a" to accept the gamble or "l" to reject it.

Good luck!
