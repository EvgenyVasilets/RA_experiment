In the following study you are about to make decisions of whether to accept a lottery or not. These lotteries will give you with equal probabilities (fifty-fifty) a positive or negative outcome. You are going to choose whether to accept or reject one a total of 24 different lotteries. The overall duration of the study should be of no more than 10-15 minutes. Each outcome in these lotteries consists of a positive or negative amount of points (1 point = 0.19 EUR). In the end, one of these lotteries will be randomly chosen and added (or subtracted) to your initial show-up fee, that you received for participating in this study.

In each trial, the lottery will be presented by showing you each outcome separately one to three times, in an alternating matter. 

One time per outcome

Two to three times per outome

After seeing the outcomes, we will ask you whether you would accept or reject this lottery. If you wish to accept, then press the upwards key (↑). In case you want to reject, press the downward key (↓). Once you have made your decision, you will see it on your screen. You will have 6 seconds to make a decision. In case you don't answer within 6 seconds, we will ask you to answer faster, and if that trial gets selected in the end, you will get the negative outcome for certain.
